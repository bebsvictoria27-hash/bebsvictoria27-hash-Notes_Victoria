The current menu-based student portal is inefficient because it is difficult to navigate and requires too many steps to complete simple tasks. Students often get confused with the layout, and it takes time to find the needed information. This causes delays, frustration, and a poor user experience.

Industry 5.0 Justification

The AI Copilot is designed to support and empower students, not replace humans. It helps by giving fast answers, clear guidance, and personalized assistance for tasks like checking clearance, viewing grades, and enrollment. Instead of removing human roles, the AI improves efficiency and allows students and staff to focus on more important responsibilities. This shows collaboration between humans and technology.

 Agile Requirements (User Stories)

As a student, I want to check my clearance using AI, so that I can save time and avoid long lines.

As a student, I want to ask about my grades through chat, so that I can easily access my academic information.

As a student, I want AI guidance for enrollment, so that I can enroll without confusion.
